I have a BS in Hospitality & Tourism Management from NYU, and enjoyed the 'different everyday' guest-facing experience in the industry. During this time, I discovered a new world of possibilities and capabilities.

I spend most of my time enjoying my hobbies and creating memories with friends. Some of my hobbies include yoga and traveling, and I like to vlog my travels sometimes to look back on, so I keep a YouTube channel just for fun.
